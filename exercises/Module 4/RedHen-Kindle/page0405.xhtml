She dearly loved fat, delicious worms and felt they were absolutely necessary to the health of her children. As often as she found a worm she would call out to her chickens:

When they were
gathered about her,

she would distribute
choice morsels of
her tid-bit.

A busy little body
was she!

Chuck-chuck-chuck!

Cheep!

CHEEP!

A busy little body was she!
